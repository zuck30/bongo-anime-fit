> **"Anime kwa Kiswahili, data kwa Kiasi — na vibe za Tetris!"**

An interactive personality quiz that matches you with an anime character based on your answers. Built with a **retro Tetris aesthetic**, **AI-powered matching** via Hugging Face, and **shareable image cards** for WhatsApp & Instagram Stories.

---

## 🎯 How It Works

1. Answer **6 fun questions** about your personality, food preferences, and social habits
2. Your answers are sent to **Hugging Face AI** (or fallback to client-side logic)
3. You get matched with an anime character: Naruto, Luffy, Zoro, Gojo, Zenitsu, Tanjiro, Levi, or Goku
4. Read your **personalized funny Swahili line**
5. **Download** your shareable character card
6. **Share** to WhatsApp, Instagram, or save to your phone

## 🎮 How to Play (Quiz Questions)

The quiz asks 6 questions in casual Swahili/English:

1. **Unapokuwa na shida kubwa, unafanya nini?**
2. **Chakula unachokipenda sana?**
3. **Marafiki zako wanakuelezeaje?**
4. **Ungekuwa na nguvu za kipekee, ungezitumia kwa?**
5. **Unapopotea barabarani (kama Zoro)...**
6. **Anime unayoipenda zaidi ni aina gani?**

Each answer guides the AI to match you with your anime personality twin.
